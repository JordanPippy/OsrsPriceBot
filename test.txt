<p>The <b>rune crossbow</b> is a <a href="/w/Crossbow_(weapon)" title="Crossbow (weapon)">crossbow</a> that is stronger than the <a href="/w/Adamant_crossbow" title="Adamant crossbow">adamant crossbow</a> but weaker than the <a href="/w/Dragon_crossbow" title="Dragon crossbow">dragon crossbow</a>. It requires a <a href="/w/Ranged" title="Ranged">Ranged</a> level of 61 to wield, and can fire up to and including <a href="/w/Runite_bolts" title="Runite bolts">runite bolts</a>. It is one handed so it can be equipped alongside a <a href="/w/Shield" class="mw-redirect" title="Shield">shield</a> or <a href="/w/God_book" title="God book">god book</a>, a great advantage for <a href="/w/Player_killing" title="Player killing">player killing</a>, as well as when fighting monsters such as <a href="/w/Dragons" class="mw-redirect" title="Dragons">dragons</a> which require an <a href="/w/Anti-dragon_shield" title="Anti-dragon shield">anti-dragon shield</a>. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.
</p><p>Rune crossbows can be made through the <a href="/w/Fletching" title="Fletching">Fletching</a> skill at level 69; a player must first cut a <a href="/w/Yew_stock" title="Yew stock">yew stock</a> for the crossbow from <a href="/w/Yew_logs" title="Yew logs">yew logs</a>, granting 50 Fletching <a href="/w/Experience" title="Experience">experience</a>. Then, a player must add <a href="/w/Runite_limbs" title="Runite limbs">Runite limbs</a> to the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 100 experience and making an <a href="/w/Runite_crossbow_(u)" title="Runite crossbow (u)">unstrung rune crossbow</a>. Finally, a player must string the crossbow with a <a href="/w/Crossbow_string" title="Crossbow string">crossbow string</a>, granting 50 experience. Creating a rune crossbow from raw materials grants 200 Fletching experience in total.
</p><p>A common method for <a href="/w/Ironmen" class="mw-redirect" title="Ironmen">Ironmen</a> to obtain a rune crossbow without the requirements to create one is by defeating the <a href="/w/Crazy_archaeologist" title="Crazy archaeologist">Crazy archaeologist</a>. The rune crossbow is commonly used among low level and Ironman accounts to complete the <a href="/w/TzHaar_Fight_Cave" title="TzHaar Fight Cave">TzHaar Fight Cave</a> due to its high accuracy, low price or ease of access, as well as its very cheap <a href="/w/Ammunition" class="mw-redirect" title="Ammunition">ammunition</a>.
</p>
